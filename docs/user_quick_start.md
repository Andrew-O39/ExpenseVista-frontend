# 🚀 ExpenseVista Quick Start Guide

Welcome to **ExpenseVista** — your personal finance dashboard.

This short guide will help you get started in minutes.

---

## 🧭 1. Sign In or Register

- Visit your ExpenseVista app.  
- Register for a new account or log in.  
- After verifying your email, you’ll land on your dashboard.

---

## 🌅 2. Welcome Page

The first time you log in, you’ll see a **Welcome Page** introducing:

- Income vs Expense previews  
- Budget tips and examples  
- Quick links to all key sections  

You can revisit it anytime from the **User Menu (top-right) → “Onboarding / Welcome Tips”**.

---

## 💰 3. Add Data

Use the **sidebar navigation** to quickly record your financial activity.

### ➕ Add an Expense
- Go to **Sidebar → Add Expense**  
- Enter category, amount, and optional notes  

### ➕ Add Income
- Go to **Sidebar → Record Income**  
- Enter amount, category, and source  

### 🎯 Create a Budget
- Go to **Sidebar → Create Budget**  
- Define category, amount, and period (weekly, monthly, etc.)

---

## 📊 4. Explore the Dashboard

Your **Dashboard** gives you a quick overview of your finances:

- **Total Income**
- **Total Expenses**
- **Net Balance**
- **Spending by category**
- **Budget vs Actual spending**
- **Income vs Expenses over time**

Charts update automatically as you add more data.

---

## 📄 5. Export Reports

You can export financial data as **PDF reports**.

Available exports:

- **Income List**
- **Expense List**
- **Budget List**
- **Budget vs Actual spending table (Dashboard)**

### How to export
1. Open any **List page**.
2. Apply filters if needed.
3. Click **Export PDF**.

The generated report will include:

- The **currently loaded data**
- **Active filters**
- **Totals summary**
- **Export timestamp**

---

## 💬 6. Finance Assistant

Click the **💬 bubble** at the bottom-right corner to chat with your **AI Assistant**.

Try asking:

- “How much did I spend this week?”
- “Am I over budget on groceries?”
- “Compare income vs expenses this month.”

The assistant can:

- Summarize spending  
- Check budgets  
- Suggest categories  
- Offer insights  

---

## 💱 7. Change Your Currency

- Click your **username (top-right corner)**.
- Select **Change Currency**.
- Choose your preferred currency (€, $, £, ₦, ¥).

Your setting is saved automatically.

---

## 🌗 8. Switch Theme

From the **User Menu (top-right)** you can choose your preferred theme:

- ☀️ Light (cream and bronze)
- 🌙 Dark (cool and modern)
- ⚙️ Auto (matches your system)

---

## 🔒 9. Stay Secure

- Each session lasts **60 minutes**.
- You’ll see a reminder before expiry.
- Use **Logout** in the **Sidebar → Account → Logout** when finished.

---

## ✅ You’re All Set!

You can now:

- Track income and expenses  
- Stay within your budgets  
- View insights on your dashboard  
- Export PDF financial reports  
- Chat with the assistant for instant answers  

Welcome aboard — take control of your finances today!
